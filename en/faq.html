FAQ

Answers to common questions about Starting Pawn and the Polyglot opening books.

What are Polyglot “.bin” opening books? ›

Polyglot opening books are compact digital libraries of opening moves in the .bin format. This binary format was originally developed for the PolyGlot program and has become a widely used standard: numerous chess programs (GUIs, engines and apps) can load and use Polyglot books.

Such a .bin book stores thousands of proven opening moves and complete lines—often compiled from master games or extensive analysis. When you use a Polyglot book in your chess software or on your e-board, the early moves of a game are played automatically from the book or suggested to you. Your engine doesn’t have to calculate in this phase; it relies on the prebuilt opening knowledge. That saves time and gets you to good middlegame positions faster. Once no book moves are available anymore (“out of book”), the engine resumes calculating on its own.

How do I use the (.bin) files on my e-board? ›

Using a Polyglot book on your e-board is simple:

  • Download: First download the desired opening book package from Starting Pawn to your computer or device. The .bin file is usually inside a ZIP archive that you need to extract.
  • Import: Open your chessboard’s software or app (e.g., the application for DGT, Chessnut or Millennium) and import the extracted .bin file as an opening book. Many programs offer a function like “Load book” or expect you to place the file in a specific folder.
  • Enable: In the software, make sure the opening book is enabled. There’s often an option like “Use opening book” or a checkbox you need to tick.
  • Start: Begin a new game. As long as you remain in a line contained in the book, moves will be played automatically from the loaded book or suggested to you or your opponent. Once the game leaves book theory, the engine or normal play mode takes over again.
Which devices/apps are supported? ›

Compatibility (.bin / Polyglot)
Our Polyglot (.bin) opening books work with popular e-boards via compatible chess software:

  • E-boards: DGT e-Boards, Millennium (ChessLink/eONE), Chessnut (Air/Pro/Evo/Go/Move), Certabo, Square Off Pro, TabuTronic, iChessOne, DGT Pegasus.
  • Software: BearChess (Windows/macOS/Linux), Lucas Chess (Windows/Linux), Arena + PolyGlot adapter, PicoChess (DGT), Acid Ape Chess (Android + DGT).

Note: The .bin books are loaded by the software, not by the board.
